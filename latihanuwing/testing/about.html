ABOUT

Haii manisss!!! Aku Dwi Hartati, biasanya dipanggil wi. Usiaku sekarang 19 tahun, aku berasal dari ukui, pelalawan, Riau, dan saat ini sedang menempuh pendidikan Sistem Indormasi di UIN Sultan Syarif Kasim Riau.

I'm a pretty easygoing person, someone who finds it easy to make friends and blend in with new people. This is very much in line with my MBTI personality type, ENFP. I enjoy socializing and being around others, but I also cherish my alone time. It’s a balance between being outgoing and appreciating moments of solitude.When I have free time, I love to entertain myself by watching movies or TV dramas. And almost every day, you'll find me listening to music. My favorites are Ariana Grande and Sabrina Carpenter—I'm absolutely in love with their songs!Besides that, I'm a good listener. I genuinely enjoy hearing people's stories. I love understanding their experiences and learning from the lessons they've gained from life. It's especially fun when my friends come to me for skincare advice. We get to learn and explore new things together, and I find that so exciting.
